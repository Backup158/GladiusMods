# 2026-02-25
Nerfed some things but minor buffs

- Massed Infantry nerfs accuracy harder by default, restoring to neutral with 2 allies. It was too easy to just be better than regular Guardsmen.
- Hold the Line increases heal rate for Valhallans near cities, instead of giving flat healing. This was a bit much.
- The Motherland Calls now gives FNP regardless, so only the additional action requires a nearby city.
