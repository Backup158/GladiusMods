Among my mods, I tend to reuse certain abbreviations and phrases. Explanations for the most common ones are listed here.
Some of these phrases carry gameplay implications. If I did things intelligently, how it works under the hood should be seamless with what the text implies, but for clarity, I'll put the specific details here.

[h2]Abbreviations[/h2]
[table]
[tr] 
[td][b]DR[/b]: Damage Reduction

[b]RDR[/b]: Ranged damage reduction
[b]MDR[/b]: Melee damage reduction
[b]WDR[/b]: Witchfire damage reduction

[b]CDR[/b]: City damage reduction
[b]IDR[/b]: Invulnerable damage reduction
[b]FNP[/b]: Feel No Pain damage reduction
[/td]
[/tr]
[/table]
