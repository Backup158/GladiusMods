Adds Jopall Indentured Squadrons (Ratling alternative) to the Astra Militarum.

Jopall Indentured Squadrons are marksmen hailing from the agri-world of Jopall, located within the Armageddon Subsector. Every person on Jopall is born into debt, due to the resources used by their birth. Each person's goal is to work until their debt is repaid, which is convenient for Guard recruiters. 

Debts can only be repaid in coin; conveniently, killing is paid in coin[spoiler]([i]Jopall's Cut[/i]: Units cost influence upkeep, but gain influence on combat. Gain rate is improved with research. Upon killing an enemy hero, gain a lump sum)[/spoiler].

They prefer defensive actions, which has made them adept at quickly setting up their counter-fire positions when in friendly territory[spoiler]([i]Urban Marksman[/i]: Ignores accuracy penalties from moving when within 3 tiles of allied cities)[/spoiler]. On the defence, they can move a short distance after firing[spoiler]([i]Reposition[/i]: Shooting only take 2 movement points when within 3 tiles of allied cities)[/spoiler].
As residents of the Armageddon Subsector, they are frequently sent to take action against Orks. As such, they've grown to quickly identify weak spots[spoiler]([i]Ork Exterminators[/i]: Increased accuracy against Orks)[/spoiler].

Jopall Indentured Squadrons can use standard Astra Militarum equipment, such as Frag Grenades, but they must pay the Quartermaster before doing so. Onlookers with keen eyes have noticed that those grenades are suspiciously plentiful, leading some to wonder if those were actually purchased from official stockpiles...[spoiler]They can use grenades without needing research.[/spoiler]
As every shot is another debt to repay, they have become expert marksmen and armed themselves with Designated Marksman Lasguns, which come with the ability to tweak the amount of charge consumed[spoiler]([u]Overcharge[/u] and [u]Undercharge[/u]: Free action to modify damage by 25%)[/spoiler].

Nothing will stop them from repaying their debts, especially not any grand ideals of comradery. Jopall Indentured Squadrons can rough up an allied infantry unit to steal their money[spoiler]([u]Shakedown[/u]: Target an adjacent allied infantry. Deal 4 hp damage and break their morale. Gains 4 influence on the next turn.)[/spoiler]. However, if a Commissar witnesses such behavior, the only payment they'll find is a bolt shell to the head[spoiler](Commissar is within 2 tiles)[/spoiler].
