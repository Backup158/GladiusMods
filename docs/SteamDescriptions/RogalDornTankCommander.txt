Adds a Rogal Dorn Tank Commander Hero to the Astra Militarum. 

[h1]Overview[/h1]
It's a Rogal Dorn Battle Tank (RDBT) with Tank Commander abilities.
Typically, RDBTs in Gladius have 1 Oppressor Cannon, 1 Autocannon, and 1 Castigator Gatling Cannon, with an additional Heavy Bolter after research. The RD Commander can choose to equip alternative weapons. 
> For the primary weapon, the Oppressor Cannon and Autocannon can be swapped for a Twin Battle Cannon, which is the typical Battle Cannon from the Leman Russ Battle Tank but [i]Twin-linked[/i], which is more effective against infantry.
> For the secondary weapon, the Castigator Gatling Cannon can be swapped for a Pulveriser Cannon, which is like a weaker Demolisher Cannon from the Baneblade, giving it greater anti-armour capabilities.
