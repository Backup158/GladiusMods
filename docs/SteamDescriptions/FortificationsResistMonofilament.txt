Monofilament weapons do less damage against Fortifications.

In Gladius, the [i]Monofilament[/i] trait gives +1 armour penetration and an accuracy increase, calculated by (accuracy * 5)/(max movement of target). This was found through community testing and is mentioned in [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3026310290]Golden Tooltips[/url]. Since Fortifications have 0 max movement (equivalent to 1 for the formula), Monofilament gives a +400% accuracy buff. Ultimately, this is a massive damage boost, which is highlighted by the [b]Warp Spiders[/b] in the Eradication Pack.
Killing a city in 2 attacks is wild, so this gives Fortifications -85% damage taken from Monofilament weapons, which also protects units such as the [b]Imperial Bastion[/b].

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
This mod is in the [b].xml.ext[/b] file format, so it will work with other mods that edit the [b]Fortifications.xml[/b] trait file. This adds a new modifier inside that trait, so having multiple mods that do the same thing will likely make the damage reduction stack.
