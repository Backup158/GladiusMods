Some abbreviations are used. They are explained [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/3670340394/767437030287488070/]in this thread[/url].

[i]Valhallan[/i]: -17% morale loss. 17% min IDR when fighting in Arctic and Tundra tiles; needs 33% more Food upkeep and takes 10% more damage when in Desert, Savannah, and Volcanic tiles. +1 armour when in Cities, Imperial Ruins, and Outposts (most units have -1 armour otherwise). +17% accuracy when fighting Orks.
[i]Massed Infantry[/i]: +1 movement when within 5 tiles of an allied city, within 2 tiles of allied Hero, or within 3 tiles of 4 other Valhallans (units with this trait have -1 movement otherwise). Accuracy is -33% when alone, -17% when adjacent to 1 ally, and neutral when adjacent to 2 or more allies. -16% morale loss when adjacent to 2 or more allies.
All Valhallan infantry can use [u]Ura![/u]: Inspires Valhallans within 3 tiles to advance with -34% morale loss, +10% FNP, +17% damage, and +10% melee attacks. Requires 4 allied infantry (where at least 2 of those are Valhallans) within 3 tiles to activate. Unit has decreased morale loss, increased damage, increased damage resistance, and increased melee attacks; in addition, they gain +23% FNP while the unit moves without attacking, to take less damage from Overwatch.
