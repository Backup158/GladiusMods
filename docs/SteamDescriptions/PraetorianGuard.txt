Adds the proud infantry of the Praetorian Guard to the Astra Militarum. 

Praetorians are renowned for their bravery [spoiler](extra base morale and [i]Praetorian[/i]: 10% more resilient to morale loss)[/spoiler] and expertise in exterminating savages [spoiler]([i]Sneering Imperialist[/i]: +17% Accuracy and +17% Ranged Attacks against Neutral units, and +10% Accuracy against Orks)[/spoiler].
While they are a bit more expensive, Praetorians operate best alongside each other [spoiler]([i]Praetorian[/i]: +1 Accuracy and +1 Morale when adjacent to another Praetorian)[/spoiler], with further benefits for Praetorian Guardsmen and Praetorian Grenadiers near each other.
