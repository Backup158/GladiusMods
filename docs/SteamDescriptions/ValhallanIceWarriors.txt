Adds the infantry hordes of the Valhallan Ice Warriors to the Astra Militarum. 

From the frozen world of Valhalla, the Ice Warriors arrive on Gladius Prime. A wave of men facing the waves of xenos, these troopers are ardent defenders, fighting to the last in the face of insurmountable odds.
When comets irreversibly shifted the planet climate to an unending winter, Valhallans were forced to adapt. Society moved into mountainous caves, many of which were connected to sprawling underground caverns. However, they had little time to adjust to this new life before the Orks landed, sparking a long, bloody struggle. Valhallans had to fight both the Greenskins and Mother Nature; in the recently emerged harsh winters, they found strength in each other. Fighting shoulder-to-shoulder until the bitter end was their primary strategy against the Orkish hordes, which also happened to be a good strategy for keeping warm.
From these early struggles, the Valhallan Ice Warriors were born. The backbone of their strategy is drowning the enemy in corpses, after softening them up with artillery. Valhallans are stronger and braver in large numbers.

[h1]Units added[/h1]
Due to text limits, specific buff values are listed in [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/discussion/3670340394/767437030287487843/]in this thread[/url].

Valhallans are experts of Arctic warfare, making them hardier in those environments but weaker in oppressively hot locales. Through tradition, they are more stubborn than typical Guardsmen, and their history lends to increased anti-Ork effectiveness. With tough times and corruption, the Ice Warriors come with brittle armour, but they compensate it by making better usage of urban cover.
While they rarely retreat, losing comrades significantly reduces their combat effectiveness. Most of these units should not be isolated.
