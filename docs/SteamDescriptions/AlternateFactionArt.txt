Changes the generic Astra Militarum faction art to Armageddon Steel Legion art.

This replaces the faction selection art, the quest art, the research completion art, and the victory screen art.

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
This mod can go anywhere in the load order.

This mod overwrites the following files in [code]Data/Video/Textures/Images/AstraMilitarum[/code]
[table][tr][td]
Faction.dds
[/td][/tr][tr][td]
FactionSmall.dds
[/td][/tr][tr][td]
Quest.dds
[/td][/tr][tr][td]
Research.dds
[/td][/tr][tr][td]
Won.dds
[/td][/tr][/table]
If another mods also overwrite these files, whichever one is on top will win.
