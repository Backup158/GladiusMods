Reduces the audio volume from using Acts of Faith for the Sisters of Battle (Adepta Sororitas).

Audio is replaced for all of them. They're still their own sounds, just quieter.
If anything reuses these sounds, they'll be quieter too.

Load order shouldn't matter, since I don't know of any other mods that replace these sound files. If there are, though, the one that is one top (visually higher) will be the one that takes effect.
